How to Choose the Right Vanity for Your Bathroom

Selecting the perfect vanity depends on your space, storage needs, and style preference. Here’s a simple guide to help you decide:

Wall Mounted Vanities

Ideal for modern homes and smaller bathrooms. They create a sleek, clutter-free look and make cleaning easier while maximising floor space.

Floor Mounted Vanities

Perfect for larger bathrooms or family homes. These vanities offer stability, generous storage, and a timeless design.

Compact Vanities

Designed for powder rooms and small spaces, compact vanities provide smart storage solutions without compromising on style.

Luxury Vanities

Statement pieces combining premium materials, elegant finishes, and ample storage for a sophisticated bathroom.

Tip: Consider the size of your bathroom, your storage requirements, and the overall style you want to achieve. The right vanity will enhance daily living.
